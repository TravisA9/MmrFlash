This is an experemntal parser to generate temporary components that will hopefully allow you to explore the text and use it in this app. You can add your text in two ways. You can just paste the Myanmar text and click Parse in the upper rigt corner. Or you can do it the pretty way.

Pretty way:
Begin by typing the word pretty and move to the next line. This is to let the app know what you want to do.

Two asterisks indicate a 'title': **ဆောင်းဦး​ရာသီမှာ
One asterisk indicates 'line' (sentence): *ဒါကြောင့် ယေရှုဟာ...
Add a translation to any 'title' or 'line' by just writing it the text on the next line:
**ဆောင်းဦး​ရာသီမှာ
In the autumn

Consecutive 'lines' (with or without translations) make a paragraph.
Add a translation to an entire paragraph with double angle brackets:
>Some long translation to the entire paragraph... . ..

Then click Parse
